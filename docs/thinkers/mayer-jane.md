# Jane Mayer

American investigative journalist (b. 1955), a staff writer for *The New Yorker* since 1995 and previously the first woman to serve as a White House correspondent for *The Wall Street Journal*. *Dark Money: The Hidden History of the Billionaires Behind the Rise of the Radical Right* (2016) documents the network of donor-funded foundations, think tanks, academic programs, and advocacy organizations built by Charles and David Koch and allied conservative fortunes, part of whose long-term program has been the reduction of taxes on capital and high incomes and the weakening of regulation. The book traces how a relatively small number of wealthy families financed an apparatus to shift American politics and policy in their economic interest. Her earlier *The Dark Side* (2008) investigated the use of torture in the "war on terror." See [[how-to-tax-the-wealthy]].

- *Dark Money: The Hidden History of the Billionaires Behind the Rise of the Radical Right* (2016)
